Hydraulic & Crane Services

At Seng Heng Group, we deliver expert hydraulic system services and crane maintenance for marine and offshore operations. Our experienced team handles the repair, testing, and servicing of hydraulic power units, winches, pumps, valves, steering gears, and control systems to ensure smooth, uninterrupted vessel functions. We work closely with shipowners and offshore operators to minimize equipment downtime and restore full operational capacity.

We also provide comprehensive crane servicing, including routine inspection, troubleshooting, and component replacement for deck cranes, lifting gear, and davits. From pressure line repairs to actuator replacements, our technicians ensure every hydraulic and lifting system operates at peak performance—whether at shipyards, on deck, or offshore locations.

Service Feature Highlights

  • We repair hydraulic equipment, including winches, pumps, motors, and actuators.
  • We maintain and troubleshoot cranes, covering deck cranes, davits, and lifting gear.
  • We perform system testing and flushing, including pressure tests and leak checks.
  • We offer 24/7 support at shipyards, onboard vessels, and offshore locations.
